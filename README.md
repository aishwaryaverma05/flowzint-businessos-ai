# FlowZint BusinessOS AI

## AI-Powered Revenue Recovery Copilot

FlowZint BusinessOS AI is an intelligent business assistant that helps companies recover overdue payments faster using AI.

Instead of only showing invoice data, the platform analyzes customer payment behavior, identifies high-risk accounts, recommends actions, generates collection workflows, and drafts professional payment reminder emails using Gemini AI.

---

## Problem Statement

Businesses often struggle to recover overdue payments.

Traditional accounting tools can show:

- Unpaid invoices
- Due amounts
- Customer records

But they do not help answer:

- Which customer should be prioritized?
- Which account is high risk?
- What action should be taken next?
- How should a professional collection email be written?

This results in delayed collections and poor cash flow management.

---

## Solution

FlowZint BusinessOS AI transforms business data into actionable insights.

The platform combines:

- Invoice Management
- Risk Analysis
- Workflow Automation
- AI Email Generation
- Business Insights
- Natural Language AI Queries

---

## Key Features

### Invoice Dashboard

Provides a real-time overview of:

- Total Due Amount
- Unpaid Invoices
- Reminders Sent
- Recovered Payments

---

### Invoice Database

Stores:

- Customer Name
- Invoice Amount
- Payment Status
- Days Overdue
- Contact Information

---

### AI Risk Analysis

Automatically classifies accounts into:

🔴 High Risk

🟡 Medium Risk

🟢 Low Risk

Based on:

- Outstanding Amount
- Payment Status
- Days Overdue

---

### Collection Workflow Generator

Creates recovery workflows such as:

Day 1 → Reminder

Day 3 → Follow-Up

Day 7 → Escalation

Day 10 → Final Notice

Day 15 → High-Risk Flag

---

### AI Email Generator

Generate professional collection emails instantly.

Example:

Draft a professional payment reminder email for ABC Ltd

---

### AI Business Insights

Provides recommendations such as:

- Highest risk customer
- Priority accounts
- Recovery suggestions
- Collection strategy recommendations

---

### Natural Language Queries

Users can ask:

Which customer should I prioritize?

Generate a collection email.

Suggest a recovery strategy.

Generate a business report.
